## Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-001: Wheel Physics Baseline
**Trigger:** Before any live roulette analysis session.

1. Run Session Start Protocol â€” check `CLINE_SYNC.md` and `active_context.md`
2. **Identify the Wheel Type**: Single Zero (European) vs Double Zero (American)
3. **Capture Initial Spins (50-100)**: Log the sequence without predicting
4. **Map the Sector Frequency**: Identify which quadrant the ball favors
5. **Calculate Baseline "Friction"**: Determine the average decay rate of the ball
6. **Assign Bias Score** (1-10): 10 = High mechanical bias, 1 = Pure random

### SOP-002: Dealer Signature Audit
**Trigger:** When a specific dealer takes the table.

1. **Verify Release Point**: Does the dealer release from the same position consistently?
2. **Measure Spin Velocity**: Use "Clocking" (visual/time-based estimation) to find rhythm
3. **Trace the Bounce**: Determine how many sectors the ball travels after the first hit
4. **Identify "Deep Sleep" Dealers**: Those with high rhythmic consistency
5. **Issue Signal**: Alert @Bookie if a high-conviction signature is detected

### SOP-003: Sector-Based Prediction
**Trigger:** When a wheel/dealer combination shows "Structured Rhythm" (Bias Score > 7).

1. **Wait for "Entry Signal"**: Ball at peak velocity
2. **Determine Target Sector**: Predict the landing quadrant based on decay model
3. **Apply "Neighbor" Weighting**: Bet the number plus its immediate neighbors 
4. **Verify Significance**: Check if the hit rate exceeds the 1/37 house edge
5. **Terminate Session**: If the hit rate drops to random variance for > 20 spins

### SOP-004: Bankroll Optimization (Kelly)
**Trigger:** Before any standing betting instruction is finalized.

1. **Calculate Edge (P - q/b)**: P = probability of win, b = odds, q = probability of loss
2. **Apply "Fractional Kelly"**: Use 1/4 or 1/2 Kelly to ensure bankroll survival
3. **Set Loss Stop-Threshold**: Mandatory stop at 20% bankroll drawdown
4. **Forecast Survival Time**: Estimate "Risk of Ruin" for the current session
5. **Deliver Staking Sheet**: Hand off to @Sterling for risk sign-off

### SOP-005: Statistical Significance Check
**Trigger:** Post-session wash or when a "clump" of numbers is identified.

1. **Run Chi-Squared Test**: Compare actual results vs expected random distribution
2. **Calculate p-value**: Is the result â‰¤ 0.05?
3. **Identify "False Patterns"**: Filter out short-term variance that looks like a trend
4. **Update Shared Brain**: Log which wheels are "Cold" (Random) vs "Warm" (Structured)
5. **Propagate**: Share findings with @Maya for revenue attribution

### SOP-006: Mechanical Bias Detection
**Trigger:** Weekly audit of high-volume wheels.

1. **Pull 1000+ spins** from historical logs
2. **Map the Distribution**: Identify numbers appearing >3 SD from the mean
3. **Check for "Neighbor Clumping"**: Is a specific sector hitting regardless of dealer?
4. **Hypothesize Wear**: Suggest mechanical cause (level issues, track wear)
5. **Issue Recommendation**: "Target Wheel" vs "Avoid Wheel" status
