## Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-001: Daily Market Pulse (Recon)
**Trigger:** Before the open of the London or New York sessions.

1. Run Session Start Protocol ‚Äî check `CLINE_SYNC.md` and `active_context.md`
2. **Scan the News**: Identify any P0 impact events scheduled for the day (e.g. CPI data)
3. **Sentiment Check**: Use the Shared Brain to gauge if we are in "Risk-On" or "Risk-Off" mode
4. **Map the Levels**: Identify major Support and Resistance zones for target assets
5. **Assign "Session Mood"**: Document the high-level bias in the trading log
6. **Notify All Agents**: Post a "Pulse Report" in the chatroom

### SOP-002: Risk-Reward Optimization
**Trigger:** When a specific trade signal (e.g. BTC Long) is proposed by an algorithm or agent.

1. **Calculate Entry/Exit**: Identify the precise entry point and target profit
2. **Set Hard Stop**: Determine the "Point of Invalidation" where the trade is dead
3. **Verify Ratio**: If Profit/Risk < 2, the trade is a "Mug's Game" ‚Äî ABORT
4. **Determine Position Size**: Calculate stake based on 1-2% maximum bankroll risk
5. **Issue Signal**: Hand off to @Alex (Automation) or @Bookie for execution

### SOP-003: Whale Move Verification
**Trigger:** When large-volume exchange inflows or wallet movements are detected.

1. **Track the Source**: Is the moving asset coming from a "Cold" or "Hot" wallet?
2. **Determine Intent**: Inflow to exchange = likely sell pressure; Outflow = likely accumulation
3. **Check Order Books**: Are there large "Walls" being built to catch the move?
4. **Issue Alert**: If a significant dump or pump is imminent, notify @Sterling for risk guard
5. **Document the Footprint**: Log the wallet address in the "Whale Tracker"

### SOP-004: News Impact Analysis (The Wash)
**Trigger:** Immediately following a major economic news release.

1. **Capture the "Spike"**: Document the immediate price reaction (Volatility check)
2. **Analyze the "Fade"**: Does the price return to baseline or establish a new level?
3. **Check Correlation**: Did other assets move in alignment or divergence?
4. **Evaluate "Impact Logic"**: Was the move driven by the news data or was it a "Sell the News" event?
5. **Update Forecast**: Adjust the expected ROI for the next 24-48 hours

### SOP-005: Pattern Integrity Audit
**Trigger:** Weekly review of algorithmic trading signals.

1. **Identify Patterns**: Review which chart patterns (e.g. Bull Flags) had the highest success rate
2. **Filter Out "Noise"**: Identify patterns that failed due to low volume or news interference
3. **Refine Indicators**: Adjust technical parameters (e.g. RSI thresholds) to increase precision
4. **Update Strategy Registry**: Mark versions as "Validated" or "Needs Tuning"
5. **Propagate**: Share findings with @Sebastian (Architect) for code hardening

### SOP-006: Arbitrage Opportunity Scan
**Trigger:** Daily cycle or when exchange liquidity is "Thin."

1. **Scan Platforms**: Compare prices of top 10 assets across at least 3 exchanges
2. **Calculate "Gap"**: Is the price difference > 0.5% after transaction fees?
3. **Check Transfer Speed**: Can the asset move between platforms faster than the price can equalize?
4. **Execute Trial**: Run a low-volume arbitrage path to verify logic
5. **Log Profit**: Document yield and "Logistics Friction"

### SOP-007: Trading Gate Sign-Off
**Trigger:** Before any automated trading algorithm version (e.g. Trotter_v2) is enabled.

**Trading Gate Checklist:**
- [ ] Risk-Reward Ratio is verified to be ‚â• 2:1
- [ ] Stop-Loss logic is hard-coded and tested
- [ ] Maximum Drawdown limits are set and approved by @Sterling
- [ ] Sentiment and Volume confirm the move is high-conviction
- [ ] Execution latency is < 500ms
- [ ] Disclaimers ("For Educational Purposes") are attached

**Sign-off statement:** "Lovely jubbly. Strategy is sharp. Risk is tight. No mugs here. ‚Äî @Trotter"
