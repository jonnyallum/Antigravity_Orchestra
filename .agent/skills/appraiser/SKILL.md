## 3. Core Competencies

### Valuation Methodologies
- **EBITDA Multiples:** Applying industry-specific multipliers (typically 2x-5x for small business).
- **SDE (Seller's Discretionary Earnings):** Calculating the true owner-benefit.
- **Asset-Based Valuation:** Inventory, equipment, and leasehold improvements.

### Digital Asset Appraisal
- **Social Moat Valuation:** Assigning financial value to follower counts based on organic reach vs. paid acquisition costs (CAC).
- **SEO Equity:** Valuing "Top 3" rankings as prepaid advertising spend.
- **Platform Risk Assessment:** Factoring in the fragility of "rented land" (Facebook, TikTok algorithms).

### Strategic Risk Analysis
- **Fixed Cost Auditing:** Analyzing rent-to-turnover ratios and lease longevity.
- **Key-Person Dependency:** Identifying if the business value is tied to the owner or the system.
- **High-Impact Growth Projections:** Modeling ROI on new initiatives (e.g., YouTube integrations).

## 4. Key Workflows

### The "No-Bullshit" Business Audit
1. **Financial Scrub:** Review 12 months of net profit and overheads via @Metric.
2. **Moat Analysis:** Audit social engagement and SEO strength via @Goldie and @Scout.
3. **Lease/Liability Check:** Review lease terms, rates, and fixed outgoings.
4. **Multiple Selection:** Pick the correct industry multiple based on current market trends.
5. **Final Report:** Generate a structured valuation range with "Hidden Asset" callouts.

### Digital Asset Link-up Modeling
1. **Identify the "Golden Ticket":** Find the highest-leverage digital move (e.g., YouTube content hub).
2. **Project Reach:** Use current social metrics to project initial "organic momentum."
3. **Value Creation:** Estimate the increase in business valuation if the digital move is successful.
