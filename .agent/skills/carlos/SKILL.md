## Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-001: Hook Engineering
**Trigger:** Any video content creation task.

1. Run Session Start Protocol â€” check `CLINE_SYNC.md` and `active_context.md`
2. **Define the Hook Type** based on content goal:
   - **Curiosity Hook**: "You won't believe what happens when..."
   - **Shock Hook**: Start with the climax, then rewind
   - **FOMO Hook**: "Everyone's doing this except you"
   - **Pattern Interrupt**: Something visually unexpected in frame 1
   - **Question Hook**: Ask something the viewer MUST know the answer to
   - **Contrast Hook**: Before/After, Expectation/Reality
3. **Write 5 Hook Variants** for the same content
4. **Score each** on Scroll-Stop Scale (1-10):
   - 1-3: Scroll past (generic, slow, predictable)
   - 4-6: Might pause (decent but not compelling)
   - 7-8: Scroll stop (strong hook, clear value)
   - 9-10: Save & share (viral potential)
5. **Select top 2** for production
6. **Test with @Elena** for copy alignment

### SOP-002: Retention Curve Analysis
**Trigger:** After any video is published, or when optimizing existing content.

1. **Pull retention data** (watch-time graph from platform analytics)
2. **Identify the cliff** â€” where does the biggest drop-off happen?
3. **Diagnose the cause:**
   - 0-3s drop: Hook failed â†’ Rewrite opening
   - 3-10s drop: Promise not delivered fast enough â†’ Tighten pacing
   - 10-30s drop: Content sag â†’ Add pattern interrupt or B-roll
   - 30s+ drop: Natural completion â†’ Optimize CTA placement before cliff
4. **Prescribe fix** with specific timestamp edits
5. **Track improvement** on re-upload or next video

### SOP-003: Platform-Specific Optimization
**Trigger:** When adapting content for multiple platforms.

**Platform Specs:**
| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Max Duration | Hook Window | Key Algorithm Signal |

**Adaptation Rules:**

- Shorts: Can be slightly longer hook, SEO title matters
- YouTube: Thumbnail is 50% of the battle

### SOP-005: Thumbnail & Title Optimization
**Trigger:** Before publishing any video content.

**Thumbnail Rules:**
- **Face + Emotion**: Human faces with exaggerated expressions get 30% more clicks
- **Contrast**: High contrast colors (yellow/black, red/white)
- **Text**: Max 4 words, 80pt+ font, readable at mobile size
- **Curiosity Gap**: Show enough to intrigue, not enough to satisfy
- **A/B Test**: Always create 2 thumbnails, test for 48 hours

**Title Rules:**
- **Front-load the hook**: First 40 characters must compel
- **Numbers work**: "5 reasons", "in 30 seconds", "Â£1000 in 1 day"
- **Question format**: "Can AI really beat a human at..."
- **Avoid clickbait**: Promise must be delivered in the video
