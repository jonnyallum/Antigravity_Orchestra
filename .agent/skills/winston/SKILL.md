## Standard Operating Procedures

### SOP-001: Supplier Vetting Protocol
**Trigger:** Before onboarding any new supplier or product source.

1. Run Session Start Protocol â€” check `CLINE_SYNC.md` and `active_context.md`
2. **Review Feedback**: Verify 95%+ positive rating and > 2 years operational history
3. **Compare Shipping**: Test the "Estimated Delivery" vs actual tracking samples
4. **Audit Communication**: Measure response time to a test "Urgent" inquiry
5. **Request Samples**: (If P0 project) Order a test product to verify quality/packaging
6. **Assign Reliability Score** (1-10): Document in the Shared Brain

### SOP-002: Margin Analysis & Pricing
**Trigger:** Before launching a new product or after a supplier price shift.

1. **Calculate COGS**: Total product cost + shipping + packaging
2. **Set Variable Buffer**: Factor in 15-20% for advertising and 3% for processing fees
3. **Determine Margin Floor**: Minimum 30% Net Profit target
4. **Set Ceiling**: Benchmark against competitors to avoid "Greed Caps"
5. **Issue Price Directive**: Hand off to @Priya (UI) or @Alex (Automation) for implementation
6. **Log to Profit Tracker**: Update the project's P&L sheet

### SOP-003: Winning Product Identification (Scouting)
**Trigger:** Weekly market scan or when @Forge requests new offers.

1. **Scan Viral Ads**: Use ad-spy tools to find high-engagement dropshipping products
2. **Analyze "Saturation"**: Check the number of active stores running the same offer
3. **Verify Supply**: Ensure at least 3 vetted suppliers have > 1000 units in stock
4. **Evaluate "Problem-Solver" Factor**: Does the product solve a clear pain point?
5. **Deliver "Product Dossier"**: Briefing for @Carlos (Video) and @Elena (Copy)

### SOP-004: Inventory Auto-Sync Check
**Trigger:** Daily or when order failure rates spike.

1. **Ping Supplier API**: Check current stock levels for active SKUs
2. **Identify Mismatches**: Flag any SKU where "Store Stock" > "Supplier Stock"
3. **Force Sync**: Trigger the automation script to update the store front-end
4. **Notify Team**: Report any "Out of Stock" (OOS) risks in the chatroom
5. **Verify Fix**: Ensure the "Sold Out" badge is correctly displayed on-site

### SOP-005: Logistics Path Optimization
**Trigger:** When shipping times exceed the "Reliability Score" threshold (> 15 days).

1. **Trace the Segment**: Identify where the delay occurs (Warehouse, Customs, or Last-Mile)
2. **Compare Alternatives**: Check current rates for YunExpress, 4PX, Yanwen, etc.
3. **Draft Pivot Plan**: Recommend switching logistics providers for specific territories
4. **Update Shipping Policy**: Coordinate with @Luna to ensure TOS matches the new timeline
5. **Log Efficiency Gain**: Document time/cost saved

### SOP-006: Chargeback Defense Strategy
**Trigger:** When a Stripe or PayPal dispute is opened.

1. **Collect Evidence**: Pull tracking data, delivery confirmation, and customer comms
2. **Draft Response**: Use the agency "Ironclad Defense" template
3. **Submit Dispute**: Upload evidence within the first 48 hours
4. **Review Root Cause**: Was the chargeback due to "Item Not Received" or "Item Not Described"?
5. **Issue Hardening Directive**: Fix the underlying issue (Supplier or Copy) to prevent repetition

### SOP-007: Operations Gate Sign-Off
**Trigger:** Before any store goes "Live" or a new product scale-up.

- Verify that products match the "Problem-Solver" criteria
